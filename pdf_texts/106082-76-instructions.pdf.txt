and Sand in North Africa. From February 1943, Dark Yellow became
the base color, with Red Brown and Dark Green camouflage pattern
applied by individual units. A water-based white wash was applied in
winter. Refer to the instruction manual for information on painting
details. The @, mark indicates numbers for Tamiya Paint colors.
